Our Favorite Local Gyms

Triangle Rock Club

Triangle Rock Club (TRC) is a popular chain of gyms in NC (and Richmond!). Depending on your focus (toprope, lead, bouldering), you may prefer one location to others. Regardless of your experience level, you'll find knowledgeable staff and quality route-setting to keep you coming back.

Progression Climbing

Progression is a smaller gym, but what it lacks in size it makes up for in quality and climbing purity. There aren't any soft grades, so don't go to Progression to get your ego stroked. Go for a real challenge and to get some tips from people who have been around the block.

Chapel Hill Community Center

The Community Center is a welcoming environment and has plenty of positive vibes. Things are a little more scrappy here, but there aren't many big egos, and it's still legit enough to host the south's oldest annual climbing competition.

OC Aerial

While climbing isn't OC Aerial's main focus, we think it deserves a place on our list. They do have some quality climbing walls, but they also have ninja warrior style courses. So you can come for climbing or some serious cross-training when you're not in the mood for yoga.
